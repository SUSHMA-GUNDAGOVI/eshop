Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer easy returns within 30 days of delivery.

Return Eligibility
Items must be returned within 30 days
Products must be unused and unworn
Original tags must be attached
Items in original packaging
Final sale items (marked as such)
Personalized/customized products
Underwear & swimwear (hygiene reasons)
Damaged items due to customer misuse

How to Return an Item

1
Initiate Return Online

Log into your account and go to "Order History". Select the item(s) you want to return and choose the reason for return.

2
Print Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label. Print it and attach it to your package.

3
Pack Your Items

Place the items in their original packaging, include all tags, and seal the box securely.

4
Drop Off Package

Take your package to any authorized shipping carrier drop-off location.

5
Track Your Return

Use the tracking number to monitor your return package. We'll notify you once we receive it.

Refund Policy

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days
Refund Timeline

Once we receive your return:

  • Processing: 1-2 business days
  • Refund Initiation: Immediately after processing
  • Bank Processing: 3-10 business days
Refund Amounts
Scenario Refund Amount Notes
Standard Return Full item price Original shipping cost not refunded
Defective/Damaged Item Full item price + shipping Includes original and return shipping
Wrong Item Shipped Full item price + shipping We cover all return costs
Partial Return Partial amount Only for returned items
Return without receipt Store credit only Current selling price applied

Exchange Policy

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color, subject to availability.

Size/Color Exchange
  • Free exchange shipping
  • Processed within 24 hours
  • Subject to stock availability
Product Exchange
  • Return original item first
  • Place new order separately
  • Price difference applied

International Returns

Important: International returns have different procedures and costs.
Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs for international orders. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

Customs & Duties

Customs fees and import duties are non-refundable. Please mark the package as "Returned Goods" to avoid additional charges.

Frequently Asked Questions
